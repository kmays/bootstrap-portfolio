About Me

Hello, my name is Kyle. I live in the bay area, where I was born and raised in the Napa Valley. I'm a full-stack developer and currently working as a freelancer in all aspects of development.

My journey into web development began with my education at UC Davis, where I earned my bachelor's degree in Science and Technology Studies. Following a stint of working at wine lab as a tech, I took my next step to furthering my web development skills by attending UC Berkeley Extension's Coding Bootcamp. My focus and passion is working with big data helping organizations answer big questions to accelerate their insights and efficiency. Outside of work I enjoy painting, finding new trails to hike, and drinking coffee while listening to a podcast.
